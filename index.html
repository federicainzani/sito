about walls This project wanted to show the barriers that men built to obstruct
others and themselves, starting from a strictly physical concept of
wall to a wider idea of barrier.
From the emblematic Berlin wall to the psychological and paralizing
wall conceived by Pink Floyd, through Anne Frank's words and social
obstacles as racial laws.

Art book by Maria Alessandra Fratta and Federica Inzani. »
